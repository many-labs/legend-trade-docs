## Referrals

- Share your referral link from the bot to invite friends
- Earn a percentage of your referees’ trading fees
- Bonuses are credited automatically to your account

## Levels

Advance through levels by trading volume and referrals to unlock lower fees and perks.

## Redeeming rewards

Rewards are tracked in your account and can be withdrawn or used to offset fees, depending on current promos.

<Tip>
Check the bot’s Referrals section for your live stats and link.
</Tip>
