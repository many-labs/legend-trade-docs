## Overview

Use these commands in any chat where the bot is present. Replace placeholder values with your own.

### Core

- `/start` — Initialize the bot and show the main menu
- `/help` — Show quick tips and links
- `/balance` — View your account balance
- `/positions` — List open positions

### Trading

- `/buy <ticker> <size>` — Market buy
- `/sell <ticker> <size>` — Market sell
- `/close <ticker>` — Close an open position

### Utilities

- `/price <ticker>` — Show the latest price
- `/history <ticker>` — Recent fills and orders

<Note>
Command names are examples; align them with the exact syntax used by your bot.
</Note>
